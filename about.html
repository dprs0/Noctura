About Noctura – Singapore’s Vibrant Japanese-Korean BBQ Experience

Welcome to Noctura, a bold reimagining of the classic Japanese BBQ (Yakiniku) and Korean BBQ experience, fused with contemporary flair and a vibrant nightlife spirit. Located in the heart of Singapore’s most iconic neighborhoods—Orchard, Clarke Quay, and Bugis—Noctura offers a one-of-a-kind all-you-can-eat BBQ feast that transcends the ordinary.

An Immersive Dining Experience

At Noctura, dining isn’t just about food—it’s about the experience. Every evening, our spaces come alive with live music, cultural performances, and karaoke nights that make each visit unforgettable. Whether you're enjoying our sizzling yakiniku grill at a cozy table or singing your heart out in one of our private rooms, the atmosphere is electric from dusk till close. Our state-of-the-art grills allow diners to enjoy premium marinated cuts—ranging from beef and pork belly to seafood and vegetarian options—cooked exactly the way they like. Paired with our signature house sauces and side dishes, this is Korean BBQ and Japanese Yakiniku like you’ve never tasted before.

All-Inclusive, All You Can Eat—For Everyone

We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy a flavorful dining experience, regardless of dietary restrictions. Our menu has been thoughtfully crafted to cater to various dietary needs:

  • Vegetarian options with clearly marked dishes
  • Allergen awareness with detailed labeling for sesame, soy, gluten, and other common allergens
  • Halal-friendly and non-pork selections for religious dietary considerations
  • Customizable sauces and marinades for guests with food allergies or other sensitivities

Our trained staff is always ready to help guests navigate the menu, and we take pride in providing a safe and welcoming environment for diners with unique dietary conditions.

Dine Your Way: Tables or Private Rooms

Whether you're looking for a casual dinner, a group celebration, or a night out with colleagues, Noctura offers multiple dining options:

  • Ordinary table reservations for intimate or group settings
  • Private rooms equipped with karaoke systems, customizable lighting, and dedicated service for a more exclusive dining experience

Our private rooms are ideal for birthday parties, corporate dinners, or simply enjoying a more private Korean-Japanese BBQ evening with friends and family.

Our Locations

Noctura is conveniently located across three vibrant districts in Singapore:

  • Orchard Road: 181 Orchard Rd, Singapore 238896 – Perfect for a post-shopping dinner or weekend feast
  • Clarke Quay: 3 River Valley Rd, Singapore 179024 – Nestled in the heart of nightlife central, ideal for dinner and entertainment
  • Bugis: 230 Victoria St, Singapore 188024 – A popular hotspot for students and tourists alikea
